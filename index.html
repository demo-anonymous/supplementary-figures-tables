Emotion-Level Alignment in Embedding Space

Figure 3. Scale-dependent alignment to emotion prototypes across happiness, anger, and sadness. We compare generated samples against both real-emotion and neutral reference groups to calibrate similarity scores. Happiness exhibits a clear monotonic trend, while anger shows weaker asymmetry and sadness requires multi-latent modulation to achieve consistent alignment, indicating emotion-dependent controllability.
